About Us

JOLINKS SOLUTIONS provides one stop automated solution for your trade and industry. Depending on the size and field of your organization, we have different products and services to meet your requirements. We provide the optimum and customized solutions made for your organization.

JOLINKS SOLUTIONS began its business operation as a hardware and network solutions providing company in August 2014. JOLINKS SOLUTIONS is focusing exclusively in high quality and cost-effective network development and implementation of services. We are advancing on a tremendous pace and with involvement of skilled and experienced people working in the organization. JOLINKS SOLUTIONS is currently doing business in Government, Banking, MNCs, Large Corporate, Telecom and in the MediaSector.

Our goal is to provide you Internet services with professionally-installed systems, monitored and maintained by our expertly-trained staff, and to ensure maximum uptime. Further, our intention is to provide equipment, service and support that will exceed expectation of our client and their guest, creating an excellent impression for our customer as well as our company.
